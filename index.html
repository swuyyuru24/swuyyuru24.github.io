My Story

How my journey into software took shape.

I’m a backend focused software engineer working on automation and integration heavy systems. I started my career building telecom purchase workflows at Infosys, where I learned how backend decisions affect reliability and customer experience at scale.

Wanting more control over implementation and deeper work with code and data led me to pursue my M.S. in Computer Science. During that time, I worked on healthcare data pipelines and dashboards at Astek Diagnostics, where reliability and data quality mattered because the software supported real decisions.

Today, I build trucking appointment automation at Qued Inc., focusing on backend services and integrations that reduce manual work and hold up in fast paced operational environments. I enjoy customer facing, problem driven work and continue strengthening my fundamentals in system design and data structures.
